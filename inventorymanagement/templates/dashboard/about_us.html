About Basma's Inventory Management System

This website is designed to help manage inventory efficiently. It allows users to browse products, add items to a shopping cart, place orders, and track receipts and invoices.

Key functionalities include:

  • Product catalog with detailed information
  • Session-based shopping cart with add, update, and remove capabilities
  • Order placement and management
  • Receipt and invoice generation
  • User profile management and authentication

This system aims to streamline inventory control and improve order processing for businesses.
